Privacy Policy

Effective: May 2025 | Last Updated: May 21, 2025

At Prisma AI ("we"), your data privacy is fundamental. This policy explains our practices.

1. Information We Collect

Account Data: Name, email address, billing information, and account preferences. Decision Data: The business metrics and constraints you submit for optimization — we process but do not retain raw data longer than 14 days. Usage Data: API request logs, recommendation metadata, and feature usage patterns.

2. How We Use Your Information

To provide and maintain our Service, improve recommendation accuracy, process payments, send important updates, and comply with legal obligations. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Retention & Security

We retain personal data for as long as your account is active. Raw decision data is deleted after 14 days. Industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3, AES-256) protects data in transit and at rest.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted partners for cloud infrastructure (AWS, Google Cloud), payment processing (Stripe), and analytics. All partners comply with GDPR/CCPA requirements.

5. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data. Contact privacy@prisma.ai to exercise these rights.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for authentication. No invasive tracking. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed in the US, EU, or other jurisdictions. We implement Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for international transfers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be notified via email or on our website.

Contact Us: Data Protection Officer at dpo@prisma.ai or mail: Prisma AI, 101 Decision Way, San Francisco, CA 94105.
